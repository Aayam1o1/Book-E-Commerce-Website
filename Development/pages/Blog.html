Technology And Its Effect On Daily Life

Blog 29 March 2022

By- Aayam Maharjan

Technology is a major factor in the lives of billons of people. Wherever we go, we see technology improving the lives of many people. However, as useful as technology is, it has a dark side that affects people in a dangerous and bad way. So, this leads us to the controversial question:Is technology a boon or a curse? and as mentioned above technology is a major factor for many peoples so what can be the effects of it in a modern day socitey?

Technology has made major positive changes in the society. Technologty has helped a lot in many ways like in the time of lockdown due to "corona virus" all the schools had been shut down which hamppered a student study a lot. But with the help of modern technolgy school were able to provide education to the students with help of online classes. Where as student can also improve there learning with help of various apps like: "My secondary teacher", "BYJU's", "Google Classroom" and even clear the confusion themselves with help of google. With the help of technology, students understand what they are taught and can apply it in real life easily. Thanks to technology, school has become a place where students love to go.

Not only in schools but technology has effects in various parts of a society. A phone is now an essential part of an individual as we can see everyone from child to an adult carrying a phone with themselves from which they can contact with their friends, relatives and many more, an individual can also kill there boredom by playing games reading books online. Its only thanks to the technology we students are able to make website like this which would be impossible without the modern technology.
